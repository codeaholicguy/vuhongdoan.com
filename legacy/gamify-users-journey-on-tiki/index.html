Tiki - Gamify users journey

How we applied game principles to grow our business

Platform
Mobile App

Year
2020
Team
PM, Engineers, and UX/UI designer

My Role
UX/UI Designer

Background
Nowadays, we can see gamification is everywhere, many apps and products are applying the principles of gamification to keep users coming back and increase their user’s engagement.

In 2020, we decided to try bringing gamification into our Tiki app to increase user engagement, happiness, and loyalty. We added more new features which could provide users a fun experience during their shopping journey, motivate them to achieve their goals, and keep them coming back.
Challenge
• How might we effectively apply gamification in our product?
• How might we keep users motivated and engaged on a regular basis?
Ideate, Iterate, Repeat
I started by researching our competitors and conducting secondary research on gamification in order to identify the most effective game mechanics. Then, after numerous brainstorming sessions, our team made a final choice on what we wanted to build at that time. The first stages should be simple but not tedious. So our initial move was to introduce the "Scratch & Win" game, in which players might receive surprising and uncertain rewards.

“If the player knows exactly what reward they are going to get for a specific action, it removes any surprise from the equation…”
 — Wendy Despain

When we were all on the same page with the product requirements, I iterated through many concepts using sketching, wireframing, and detailed flows, and discussed them with the team to gain input for improvements.

Wireframing ideas for Scratch & Win

Hi-fi Design
I moved forward with the process to the hi-fi phase after validating the wireframes. The game mechanics that we applied would be as follows:
I — A 5-day streak
Users have a clear goal to achieve, they have to enter a state flow to win a prize. Plus, it helped us to increase users engagement and retention.
II —​​​​​​​ Unpredictable reward
Users could randomly feel rewarded through their shopping journey by completing a daily quest, it can be: Visit a campaign page, Add a product to cart, or Follow a store. And by then, they will receive a scratch card as an unpredictable reward.
III — Badges
When users complete multiple quests, win multiple prizes or earn multiple badges, they will be awarded the badges. It helps us to users know that their success is noticed and appreciated, which in turn can often spur them on toward even greater achievements. 
Measure the success
• The retention rate of players is significantly higher than this rate of non-players. About 42% of users who play the game returned to the app, compared to only 4% of non-players.
• Our data also indicated that players are much more active and spend more time on the app than non-players.
• Comparing the conversion funnel, the conversion rate is 53.3% of players compared to 18.3% of non-players.
