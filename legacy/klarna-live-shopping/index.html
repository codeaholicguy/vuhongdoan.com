Klarna - Live Shopping

Connecting merchants and consumers offers an interactive and real-time shopping experience.

Platform
Mobile (Klarna app)
Year
Late 2023 – 2024
Team
Stakeholders, PM, Engineers, Product Designer

My Role
Sole Product Designer
Background
From our regular talks with merchants, it became clear that despite the success of the Klarna Store App, which allows them to have direct communication with their customers through chat or video calls, there is still a need for merchants to be able to engage with a larger audience of customers more proactively.
As part of Klarna's vision to become a shopping destination, our team wanted to explore a new initiative that would allow merchants to connect with their customers on a larger scale.

We found that live shopping is quite popular in some regions (particularly Asia) but its adoption in the EU and North America seems to be relatively limited. We identified a chance to bring a comparable interactive shopping model to these markets and set Klarna apart in the competitive Buy Now, Pay Later industry.
Objectives
Our goal was to create a seamless live shopping experience that would revolutionize how merchants and consumers interact, driving both engagement and sales. Therefore it will help Klarna to:
Strengthen merchant relationships
By offering a platform for live shopping, we aimed to build stronger relationships with merchants. This involved providing them with tools to showcase their products in real-time with mass shopper audiences and ultimately increasing product sales.

We envisioned this product as a way for them to create more personalized and engaging shopping experiences, enhancing their brand presence and loyalty.
Inspire users to buy more via Klarna app
This was expected to drive repeat purchases and increase user stickiness, as shoppers would be more likely to return for future events and explore other offerings on the Klarna app.
Gain deeper insights into Shopper behavior
This will provide real-time data on shopper preferences, shopping behaviors, and purchase patterns. We can leverage this data to gain a deeper understanding of our users, which would inform future product developments and strategies.
Key learnings
Initially, we didn’t have a clear mission or specific goals for the live streaming - live shopping experience. With no prior insights, my team and I initiated discussions with merchants to understand their needs better. We also worked closely with our partners to explore how they leveraged live streaming to encourage connections and engagement between brands and customers.
Our research involved studying existing live streaming platforms and understanding the needs of both merchants and consumers. Here are some key insights we gathered:
I - Merchants' goals with Live events

🛍️  Boost consumers' purchase intentions
Merchants found live streaming to be an effective way to advertise their products, ongoing promotions, and special events. Real-time engagement and direct interaction with viewers often lead to instant sales and increased participation in promotional activities.


💟  Enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty
Brands like Sage use live streams to offer master classes or tutorials, demonstrating how to use their products effectively. These sessions help them enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty, reduce return rates, and increase long-term engagement with the brand.
II - Challenges they face with existing platforms
1. Overwhelming content
Both viewers and merchants face difficulties with content discoverability.
•   Merchants struggle to stand out and gain visibility, as they have to compete with a wide range of content, not just shopping-related streams.
•   Viewers struggle to find relevant events that match their interests as there are so many different types of video content on these platforms. It's challenging for viewers to stay focused on a single stream without being tempted to swipe to other content
💡 Opportunity
Compared to social media platforms, our Klarna app is designed to streamline the shopping experience by focusing solely on shopping-related content.

This allows merchants to effortlessly connect with their target audiences and helps viewers find the video content they enjoy more easily.
2. Complex checkout process
The existing platforms required viewers to leave the live stream and be redirected to an external web/app to complete purchases, which sometimes disrupted the shopping flow and increased the chance of shopping cart abandonment.
💡 Opportunity
We can leverage the in-app browser in the Klarna app eco-system to provide a seamless transition for viewers to the checkout process on merchant websites, eliminating the need to switch to another app.
Kick-off
Given the complexities and technical demands of developing such an in-house system, after thorough consideration, we decided to partner with an established live-streaming provider to deliver a live shopping solution for our merchants. This would enable us to leverage their expertise and existing infrastructure to integrate a seamless video player into the Klarna app for hosting and streaming live broadcasts. 
By utilizing the live-streaming provider's event management software and data reporting tools for merchant experience, we could focus our resources on building consumer experience within the Klarna app.

Design and Iterations
I - Live event and Shopping experience
To address the insights gathered from our research, I discussed with the team to prioritize the features built for the first release, and started exploring the design for this feature with the following goals:
🛍️  Seamless shopping flow from discovery to checkout
Allow users to smoothly discover the products from the live stream and make a purchase within the Klarna app. This aims to reduce friction and enhance the overall shopping experience.

💬  Enhance interaction
Foster viewer engagement and interaction during live shopping events.

___________________
1st iteration: Briefly introduce all the products that will be presented
This approach ensures a seamless shopping flow from discovery to making a purchase. Once viewers navigate to the merchant's website, the live event closes to avoid any distractions and focus their attention solely on buying products.

___________________
2nd iteration: Highlight the product being showcased in real time
After analyzing feedback for the first design, I learned that:
•   It was difficult for viewers to find a certain product that the host was presenting
•   Some viewers might want to continue watching the video while shopping

Therefore, I decided to focus more on the product currently being presented in real-time to maintain engagement and provide a seamless experience.
With this design, viewers can interact with this highlighted product and also have a way to discover all other products being showcased. When viewers land on the merchant's website, the live event minimizes to a small view, allowing them to continue watching the live stream while browsing the website. This can be closed or expanded if desired, giving users flexibility in how they want to interact with the live event while shopping.
II - Live events discovery
I talked with other designers to explore the potential entry points for Live events and started drafting ideas on how users can explore the Live content with a primary goal:
🎯  Personalized content discovery
Improve content discoverability to help viewers find relevant events and assist merchants in standing out.
Proof of concept
We worked with selected cosmetics brands and beta-launched this product for the US market in Dec 2023 - 5 months after we kicked off the project.

To ensure that the first live events could reach a wide range of target audiences, we worked closely with brands and the Klarna Ads team to promote the launch beforehand.
What I've learned after the beta launch
User interest vs. Actual participation
A significant number of users showed interest in the events before they went live, but only about 30% of these interested users actually joined the live events.


This indicated the need for better strategies to convert interest into action, such as more engaging pre-event content, a better reminder feature, or incentives for joining the live event.

___________________
Viewer retention challenges
During the first live events, about 80% of viewers dropped off within the first 10 minutes since joining.


The high drop-off rate shows that the content or presentation may not have been compelling enough to retain viewers.

The first few minutes since joining are important for capturing and retaining viewer interest, and this relies heavily on the quality of the content and the presenters.

___________________​​​​​​​
Navigational challenges
Users interested in multiple products had to navigate back and forth between different pages to make a final purchase.


This disrupts the seamless shopping experience, leading to the risk of cart abandonment and a potential loss of sales.

✨ Future directions

Our ultimate goal is to enhance the live shopping experience and make it as seamless as possible. In order to align with the overall vision and incorporate what we've learned since the launch, I started working on iterative changes to the design.

The focus was on implementing a more integrated shopping cart experience within the live stream, enabling viewers to easily and seamlessly add products to their cart directly without navigating to other pages.

Seamless checkout integration

This eliminates the need to navigate to different pages, providing a seamless shopping journey. With just a simple tap, viewers can check out all selected products directly on the merchant's website, ensuring a fast and convenient purchasing process.

This streamlined approach might not only enhance user satisfaction but also help boost conversion rates for merchants, making live shopping a highly efficient and enjoyable experience for all parties involved.
Reflection
As this was just beta-launched, there is plenty of room for improvement based on user feedback and performance metrics.

I also understand that the success of a live event primarily relies on the quality and appeal of the content itself. Thus, ongoing collaboration with merchants to enhance their live presentations will be key to maximizing the platform's potential and achieving sustained success.
