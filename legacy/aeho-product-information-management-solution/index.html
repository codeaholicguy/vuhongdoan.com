Aeho - Product Information Management (PIM)

A solution that helps companies manage and distribute large quantities of product data

Platform
Desktop Web App

Year
2019
Team
Founders, Engineers, UX/UI designer

My Role
UX/UI Designer
Background
Aeho is a Product Information Management Software, which is an enterprise tool used to gather, manage, and distribute product data across multi-channels. The target customers were considered as Small and Mid-size Enterprises in Vietnam based on the business strategy.

I joined the team at the early stage of this project. Along with the founders, we defined the product requirements and what we intended to develop for the MVP.
Discover
I started to gather the key insights we had from the business research on our competitors, our target customers, and stakeholders who were in charge of product information management. It was important to me to understand their current daily working process and identify their pain points.

Key insights
• Many stakeholders will involve in the process, it could be brand manager, content writer, marketer, partner, etc.)
• The manual process leads to contain errors and is difficult to scale
• Difficult to share multiple versions of information with different suppliers and partners
Define
Aeho was designed to ease the process of creating, managing, and distributing product information. The discovery phase helped us define the scope, which was narrowed down to 3 main goals as follows:
1. Automation the process— Users can manage their rich data and distribute into across multi-channels, all in one place
2. Shareability — Allow the company to securely share product information with their partners; and grant the different levels of access to users
3. Ease to use —Create an interface and a process that non-tech users can easily follow
"Centralize and enrich the integrality of products information into a single digitized platform"
Exploration & Ideation
After we all aligned with the goals, I moved forward to the next step by listing down all the main tasks and creating a diagram that helps organize all of our content, how a user moves from one page to another.

Information Architecture

How users can access the tool? 
At the MVP stage, new users will not be able to register with the system and will only be able to access the tool from email invitations. Our signed customers' administrators must log into Aeho system to grant access to others (their internal members & partners).

What data can be managed?
• Basic data: names, SKUs, titles, descriptions, variants, categories, price…
• Images, Videos
• Product resources (instructions, guides, quality labels…)
• Marketing and sales documents
• SEO settings
• And more
Design & Iteration
Based on the established points above, I started with sketches to explore ideas, then transferred them into wireframes to discuss with the founding team and gather feedback for iterations. Here are some common actions that users may perform in Aeho:
Bulk import & validate product data
Users can quickly bulk import their product data if a new set of products is released or thousands of updates to current data are required.
The data validation helps to:
• Verify that the data meets the given standards and prevents data import errors
• Connect the incoming data to the relevant existing product
• Avoid product duplicate creation
Centrally manage product data
Users can control and manage their data more productively and efficiently in one single place, for all products and sales channels.
Final Design
After several rounds of iterative design and testing, I finally arrived at a solid solution. I wanted to make the UI be clean and minimal but vibrant to reflect the brand's personality.
